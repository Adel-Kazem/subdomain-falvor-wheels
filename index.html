OUR STORY

CULINARY INNOVATION ON WHEELS

Flavor Wheels was born in 2018 when Chef Alex Rodriguez left his position at a Michelin-starred restaurant to pursue his dream of making exceptional food more accessible to everyone.

Our mission is simple: combine the quality and creativity of fine dining with the fun, casual atmosphere of street food. We source locally whenever possible and change our menu seasonally to ensure the freshest ingredients and most innovative flavors.
